About Us

Split bills applications are mobile or web-based tools designed to simplify the process of dividing expenses among a group of people.

These apps are particularly useful when friends, roommates, or colleagues want to split the costs of shared expenses, such as meals, rent, utilities, or travel expenses. Instead of manually calculating and tracking each person's share, these apps automate the process and provide a convenient platform for managing shared expenses.
